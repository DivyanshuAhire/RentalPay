RentalPay Platform Guide
Welcome to your platform! This guide explains how the website works, the automated processes, and the manual tasks you need to perform as the owner/admin.

1. High-Level Website Workflow
The platform operates on a Trust-and-Verify (OTP-based) model to ensure items are safely exchanged and payments are secured.

Step-by-Step Flow:
Onboarding: Users sign up and verify their phone numbers via OTP (Firebase).
Listing: A user (Owner) uploads an item. It goes to Pending status.
Moderation (Manual): You (Admin) review and approve the listing.
Booking: A user (Renter) pays for an item (Rental Fee + Security Deposit + Platform Fee).
Handover (Automated Security):
The Renter has a Pickup OTP.
The Owner asks for this OTP when they meet.
Once the Owner verifies it, the Owner's earnings are unlocked.
Return (Automated Security):
The Owner has a Return OTP.
The Renter asks for this OTP when they return the item.
Once the Renter verifies it, their Security Deposit is unlocked.
Withdrawal: Users request their "Available" funds (Earnings or Deposits).
Payout (Manual): You (Admin) send the money to their bank/UPI and mark it as completed.
2. 💰 Financial Flow (How Money is Handled)
It is crucial to understand that the system holds the money as a middleman until the exchange is verified.

The Breakdown:
When a renter pays ₹1000 for a rental:

Total Paid: ₹1000 (Example: ₹700 Rent + ₹200 Deposit + ₹100 Platform Fee).
Platform Commission: The system automatically takes 15% from the Rent (₹700 - 15% = ₹595 for the owner, ₹105 for you).
Security Deposit: The ₹200 is held by you (the platform) and is 100% refundable to the renter if the item is returned safely.
The Stages of Money:
Pending: When the renter pays, the money is "Locked" by the platform. Neither the owner nor the renter can withdraw it.
Available (Owner Earning): Becomes available ONLY after the Pickup OTP is verified. This ensures the owner only gets paid if they actually handed over the item.
Available (Security Deposit): Becomes available ONLY after the Return OTP is verified. This ensures the renter only gets their deposit back if they actually returned the item.
Requested: When a user clicks "Withdraw", they are asking you to send this "Available" money to their actual bank account.
Completed: This is the final stage after you manually transfer the money and click "Confirm" in the Admin Panel.
3. Automated Processes (The System Does This)
OTP Generation: Unique 6-digit codes for every pickup and return.
Status Transitions: Moving money from "Pending" to "Available" based on OTP verification.
Commission Calculation: Automatically splitting the rent between the owner and the platform.
Payment Tracking: Marking orders as "Paid" once the renter completes the gateway transaction.
4. Manual Tasks (Things YOU Must Do)
A. Navigation & Dashboard
Use the Sticky Navigation Bar at the top of the 
Admin Dashboard
 to quickly jump between:

Moderation: Reviewing new listings.
Payouts: Handling money transfers.
Users: Managing members.
Transactions: Viewing recent sales.
B. Listing Moderation
Review Pending listings.
Approve if they meet quality standards.
Edit if you need to fix a small detail (price/category) before making it live.
C. Processing Payouts (The Most Important Task)
Go to the Payout Requests section.
Look for "Requested" cards.
Check Details: The card shows the User's Bank Account or UPI ID.
Transfer Funds: Open your bank/UPI app and send the exact amount shown on the card.
Confirm: Click "Confirm Payment Sent" to remove the request and notify the user.
5. Security Features
Middleman Security: The platform holds all funds until the physical exchange is verified by both parties.
OTP Verification: Prevents "I never received the item" or "I returned it" disputes.
Security Deposit: Provides a buffer for the owner against damage.
IMPORTANT

Pro Tip: Always verify the Beneficiary Name in your bank app matches the name shown on the payout card before sending money.
