Shipping & Self-Pickup Policy – RentalPay

Effective Date: May 2026
Platform: RentalPay

1. INTRODUCTION

This Shipping & Self-Pickup Policy explains how item collection and return currently operate on RentalPay.

At present, RentalPay does NOT provide:

Courier services
Home delivery
Logistics handling
Shipping partnerships
Packaging services

All rentals currently operate through direct self-pickup and self-drop arrangements between users.

By using the platform, users agree to this Policy.

2. CURRENT DELIVERY MODEL

RentalPay currently supports ONLY:

✅ Self Pickup

The renter collects the item directly from the owner or agreed meeting location.

✅ Self Return / Self Drop

The renter returns the item personally after the rental duration ends.

RentalPay does NOT currently offer:

Doorstep delivery
Courier pickup
Third-party shipping
Platform-managed transportation

Renters are responsible for:

Arriving on time for pickup/drop

B. Owner Responsibilities

Owners are responsible for:

Providing accurate pickup details
Making items available as agreed
Ensuring items reasonably match listing descriptions
Cooperating during pickup/return coordination
